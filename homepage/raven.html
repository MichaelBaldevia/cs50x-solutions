Raven focus on meelee to mid range attacks. Unlike ripper, most of raven's skills are "throw and forget" type. It is rather easier to play compared to ripper who requires close melee range to dish out the damage. However, raven's side of skill tree lacks damage and therefore is very reliant of the burst damage under the effect of fade. It is recommended to build a hybrid raven rather than going for pure raven build.

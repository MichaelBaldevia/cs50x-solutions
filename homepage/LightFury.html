Although it is said they act as a healer/supporter, Light Furies are also capable of attacking efficently as their overall damage as a Bringer helps them. Do note that when gearing your Light Fury, it is recommended that they are gears to for survivability instead of DPS as Light Furies main role is still to support instead of damage. Therefore, try to get equipment and plates which are able to increase HP and defense.
